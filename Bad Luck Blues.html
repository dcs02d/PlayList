Bad Luck Blues

Recorded by Blind Lemon Jefferson in 1926

September 24, 1893 - December 19, 1929

Jefferson died in Chicago at 10:00 a.m. on December 19, 1929, of what his death certificate said was "probably acute myocarditis".

For many years, rumors circulated that a jealous lover had poisoned his coffee, but a more likely explanation is that he died of a heart attack after becoming disoriented during a snowstorm. Some[who?] have said that he died of a heart attack after being attacked by a dog in the middle of the night.

OR he was killed while being robbed of a large royalty payment by a guide escorting him to Chicago Union Station to catch a train home to Texas. Paramount Records paid for the return of his body to Texas by train, accompanied by the pianist William Ezell.
